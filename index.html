The Good Guys BC

I am a Chapter President at The Good Guys BC Foundation. I lead a group of passionate individuals in making a difference in our community. From workshops and school-wide events to care package assemblies and sandwich distributions, we work to educate Canadian youth on the topic of homelessness. In my time as president, my team has raised over $2000 for those experiencing homlessness in The Lower Mainland.
